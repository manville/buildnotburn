# Lemon Squeezy Branding Guide

Use these settings in your Lemon Squeezy dashboard under **Settings > Design** to match the look and feel of your BuildNotBurn application.

- **Background:** `#1a1a1a`
- **Header:** `#1a1a1a`
- **Logo:** Upload your `logo.png`
- **Headings:** `#FFFFFF`
- **Primary Text:** `#CCCCCC`
- **Secondary Text:** `#888888`
- **Links:** `#FF7700`
- **Borders:** `#333333`
- **Focus State:** `#FF7700`
- **Button:** `#FF7700`
- **Button Text:** `#000000`
